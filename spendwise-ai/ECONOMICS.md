# Economics

## What a Converted Lead is Worth to Credex

Credex sells discounted AI infrastructure credits — Cursor, Claude, ChatGPT Enterprise sourced from companies that overforecast. Assume:

- Average credit purchase: $5,000 (a team buying 6 months of Claude Team at a 30% discount vs retail)
- Credex gross margin on credits: ~20% (discount arbitrage business)
- Gross profit per purchase: $1,000
- Repeat purchase rate within 12 months: ~50% (teams that get value renew)
- LTV over 18 months: $1,000 + (50% × $1,000) = **$1,500 per converted customer**

This is conservative. Enterprise deals ($20k+) exist but aren't the base case for a tool that attracts seed-stage teams.

## Conversion Funnel That Makes This Profitable

```
1,000 visitors
  → 400 start audit (40% — free tool, low friction)
  → 250 complete audit (62.5% completion — form is short)
  → 100 email captures (40% — shown after value is delivered)
  → 20 high-savings audits (>$500/mo) get Credex CTA prominently surfaced
  → 4 book consultation (20% of high-savings leads)
  → 2 purchase credits (50% close rate from consultation)

Revenue per 1,000 visitors: 2 × $1,500 LTV = $3,000
CAC at zero paid budget: ~$0 cash
ROI: effectively infinite until paid channels are added
```

The bottleneck is **consultation close rate** — 50% is aggressive for cold leads. At 25%, the math still works:
- 1 purchase per 1,000 visitors → $1,500 revenue
- At $500 CAC (paid ads to drive 1,000 visitors) → $1,000 profit per 1,000 visitors

Break-even on paid acquisition: CAC < $1,500 (lifetime value). This gives significant headroom.

## What Would Have to Be True for $1M ARR in 18 Months

Credex needs to close ~667 credit purchases at $1,500 average gross profit each (or fewer at higher deal sizes).

**Scenario A — Volume model (seed-stage teams, $5k deals):**
- 667 purchases × $5,000 average deal = $3.3M in credit sales
- At 20% margin → $667k gross profit (not quite $1M, needs either more volume or higher margin)
- Requires: 667 / 18 months = ~37 purchases/month
- At 2 purchases per 1,000 visitors → need 18,500 visitors/month
- Achievable with 2–3 viral Show HN / newsletter mentions per month + light paid spend

**Scenario B — Enterprise mix (one $20k deal per month):**
- 12 enterprise deals × $20,000 = $240k + 500 seed deals × $5,000 = $2.5M
- Combined gross profit at 20% margin: $548k + lighter paid volume from tool
- The audit tool qualifies enterprise inbound; sales closes them
- Requires: audit tool surfaces companies with >$5k/mo AI spend → direct outreach by Credex sales

**Most realistic path to $1M ARR:**
The tool drives top-of-funnel awareness and qualifies leads by savings size. Credex sales focuses on the top 10% of audits (teams spending >$3k/month on AI tools) where a Credex credit purchase has clear, immediate ROI. At 50 such leads per month from the tool, closing 4–5/month at $8k–15k average deal size → $40k–75k MRR → $500k–900k ARR within 12 months. $1M ARR requires either higher close rates, larger deals, or a second acquisition channel beyond the audit tool.

**Key assumption to stress-test:** The 40% audit completion rate. If it drops to 20% (form is too long, users distrust the data), the entire funnel halves. The form must be fast — under 2 minutes for a 3-tool stack.
